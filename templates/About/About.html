About HealthMate
HealthMate is a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application designed to provide quick and accurate health insights. Leveraging a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model, our platform allows users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses instantly.

The Problem
People often experience symptoms and seek immediate answers about their health. Differentiating between diseases can be challenging due to overlapping symptoms.

Solution
HealthMate addresses this by allowing users to input symptoms through a user-friendly interface. Our Decision Tree Classifier, trained on a dataset of symptoms and corresponding diseases, predicts the most likely illness. Users receive detailed information about the predicted disease, including descriptions, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Key Features
User-Friendly Interface: Easy symptom input.
Accurate Predictions: Reliable disease predictions using machine learning.
Comprehensive Information: Detailed disease info with recommendations.
Educational Content: Informative health blog posts.

Conclusion
HealthMate combines technology and healthcare to provide accessible and reliable health information, enhancing health awareness and improving well-being.
